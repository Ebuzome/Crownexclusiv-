Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use CrownExclusiv skincare products?

Most CrownExclusiv products are designed for daily use. For best results, we recommend using cleansers and toners twice daily, while serums and creams should be applied once or twice daily depending on your skin type.

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes, we offer a range of products specifically formulated for sensitive skin. All our formulations are dermatologist-tested and free from harsh chemicals. We recommend patch testing new products before full application.

How long does shipping take within Nigeria?

We offer express shipping to major cities within 24-48 hours. For other locations, delivery typically takes 3-5 business days. All orders are processed within 24 hours of payment confirmation.

Can I return products if they don't suit my skin?

We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return unopened products for a full refund or exchange. Please contact our customer service for assistance.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within Nigeria. However, we're working to expand our shipping options to other West African countries in the near future.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email. You can track your order status through our website or by contacting our customer support team via WhatsApp.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Absolutely! CrownExclusiv is committed to cruelty-free beauty. None of our products or ingredients are tested on animals at any stage of production.
